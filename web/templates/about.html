What is bakkify?

Bakkify is an app that analyzes how religious people use social media. It's based on a series of posts from my blog.

How does bakkify work?

Bakkify searches tweets from twitter's public stream for Bible verse references. Next I perform some offline analysis to correlate "topics" to specific Bible verses ranked by relevance.

Why are you doing this?

I want to know why certain people have an affinity toward certain scriptures. Lots of Christians use twitter.com to evangelize, so its a good place to start mining for patterns in Bible usage.
